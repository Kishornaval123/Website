About US

Journey of Polywell to achieve this glory in waste management market

Since its founding in 1989, Polywell has grown from a small manufacturer to a leading name in waste management products. Over the decades, the company expanded its product line to include bio-hazard bags, pedal dustbins, wheeled dustbins, and sharp containers, becoming known for its quality and innovation.

By forming strategic partnerships and responding swiftly to global challenges, such as the COVID-19 pandemic, Polywell solidified its reputation. Now celebrating 35 years, Polywell continues to lead the industry with a commitment to excellence and sustainability.
